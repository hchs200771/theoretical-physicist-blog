A personal academic site for **Yi-Hsien (Sherry) Du**, a theoretical physicist.

## About

Sherry is a Postdoctoral Fellow at **MIT**, supported by the Simons Collaboration on
Ultra-Quantum Matter. She is a theorist working on **emergent collective phenomena** in
quantum matter — connecting deep theoretical structures to real experimental phenomena.

Her research sits at the interface of **quantum field theory** and **strongly correlated
many-body systems**, with a focus on:

- **Quantum Hall states** — geometric and gravitational responses, chiral gravitons, and the
  effective theory of fractional quantum Hall fluids.
- **Moiré quantum materials** — skyrmion Chern bands and emergent topological structure in
  twisted two-dimensional materials.
- **(Non-)Fermi liquids** — controlled theories of metallic states beyond the Landau paradigm.

She earned her Ph.D. in Theoretical Physics at the **University of Chicago** (advisor: Dam Thanh
Son), with earlier training at Imperial College London and a KITP graduate fellowship at UC
Santa Barbara.

## About this site

A single-page static site — no framework, no build step.

```

style.css    # styling, light/dark via [data-theme]
photo.jpg    # hero photo
```

Edit the files and push to `main`; GitHub Pages redeploys within a minute. To preview locally,
open `index.html` directly or run `python3 -m http.server 8000`.
